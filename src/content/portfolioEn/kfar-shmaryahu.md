# House in Kfar Shmaryahu

This project was designed for a young family seeking a home that feels open, bright, and minimal.
Natural materials such as oak wood and concrete create an atmosphere that is both warm and contemporary.

## The challenge

The main challenge was creating privacy on a corner lot while preserving the strongest possible openness toward the garden.
